This is my Sugar Cookie recipe, it has been in my family for many generations!

This recipie containing very less ingredients they are:

  1. Butter
  2. Sugar
  3. Flour

Instructions


  1. Make the dough: beat together butter, sugar, and flour until blended.
    ,
  2. Form the cookies: form the dough into 1-inch balls. Roll the balls in sugar, then flatten them with a glass or measuring cup.
  3. Bake: bake until just barely golden around the edges and bottom.
  4. Rest: let rest and cool for at least 10-15 minutes before eating.

Thank you, and I hope that you enjoy your cookies.
